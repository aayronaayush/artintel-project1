How it works

Train Your Own Smart Self-Meditation And Nutritionist Agent With AI Powered Neural Networks To Enhance Your Work Out Activities.

We know your next move

All your work out activities and body movement will be tracked and stored using Kinect camera device on our online servers for further preprocessing of data. An RGB color, VGA video camera, a depth sensor, and a multi-array microphone all work together to detect your motion with the Kinect Camera. The red, green, and blue color components are captured by the camera, as well as the user's body type, and face characteristics. The right workout technique is the way to reaching your goals without injuries, which is where the Kinect camera comes into use as it not only fetches and tracks out your workout sessions, but it also warns the user if a wrong technique was detected. Collecting data from the body’s movement is to assist the user to reach their goals efficiently and safely. Our smart AI neural networks algorithm keeps an eye on joint and muscle movement to further enhance the body parts health using our smart meditation technology and nutrition recommendations.

SMART GUIDED SELF MEDITATIONS

Our AI deep neural network and computer vision algorithm helps you to self-meditate your body. The app recommends to the user and helps visualize the best self-meditation practices to follow up with the previous workout. Based on the movements of the body, the self-meditation smart agent guides you on the best fitting meditation techniques, to maximize the benefits of an intensive workout. Meditation, after working out, has been proved to reduce stress, shorten recovery time, relieve pain, lower your blood pressure, and battle anxiety. The meditation options differ based on the user’s needs ranging from as basic as deep breathing to as complicated as Yoga.

NEED BASED OPTIMIZATION OF YOUR EVERY MEAL

Planning out what to eat before and after a workout can be such a struggle. Introducing our next gen AI enabled app to your workouts would fix that as the machine learning technology will serve your next healthy meal according to your body's requirements. The app works with the data received from the tracked body movement during work out and your set goal to provide you with the perfect meal to reach your full capacity. The smart daily diet scheduling based on your daily work outs, and goal would increase stamina, muscle growth, and fat burn, along with maintaining the body’s needs. Having the specific amounts of proteins, carbohydrates, and healthful fats fit to your needs are all essential for the body’s recovery after a workout.
